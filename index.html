How To Play

      
Each day gives you 2 puzzles. Clear Easy first, then Hard unlocks. Drag the 4 correct tiles into the Venn diagram and the board checks automatically when your last tile lands.

      

        
1. Each spot belongs to a different overlap of the 3 categories.

        
2. Correct tiles lock in place. Wrong tiles return to the pool after the board checks.

        
3. Each puzzle gives you 3 tries. Hard can get one extra lifeline try if you need it.

      

      

        
        
      

    

  


  

    

      
Keep Your Streak

      
You are out of tries on Hard. Use your one extra lifeline try to protect today's run.
